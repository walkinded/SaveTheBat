A brave bat named Rina is fighting evil for justice. Help the brave bat to pass all the challenges and reach the cherished goal!

Characters

Rina

A brave, strong bat, with a glossy grayish skin, piercing blue eyes, sharp ears and clingy feet.

Horrsturm

Ambitious corgi, with a bad temper and evil intentions. And the thing is that once, the kingdom, which owns all three worlds, did not elect Horrsturm as Emperor. And now he takes revenge on the ungrateful inhabitants of various eras, putting them to the test.

Raville

Elder, guardian of the city of Friedelmausville. Just, full of reasoning, old man. Does magic, protects bats.
